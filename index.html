Luneta Park

Freedom Park (formerly Luneta Park) is located near the seashore, enticing the visitors with its soothing and relaxing ambiance and touch of the cool,fresh south winds.Beacuse of its great potential of becoming a crowed drawer in terms of tourist spots.

Poctoy White Beach

Torrijos White Beach (Poctoy White Beach Resort), famous for its fine and powdery white sands, is the one of the finest beaches in the province of Marinduque. It is considered by the local and foreign tourist as one of which surpasses all other beach resort in terms of location.
